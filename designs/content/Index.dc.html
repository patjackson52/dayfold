Content types — Now, Hub & Detail
Dayfold · the content library · ADR 0015

A library of content, surfaced where you need it

The file, link, invite, contact, map or email you'd otherwise hunt across a dozen apps — filed by Claude as a typed content item. Each type renders the same three ways: a glanceable card in Now, a block in a Hub, and a full Detail on tap, opened with a Material 3 Expressive container transform. The use case drives the data and the trigger that decides when it shows.

A content item is just data + a type; the app picks the card, block and detail layout from the type. New use cases — tickets, receipts, boarding passes — slot in by adding a type, not a new screen. The detail's anatomy is constant: hero (the content itself), metadata, actions, provenance, related.
