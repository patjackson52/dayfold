The thing you need, surfaced where you need it

The problem: the form, the Drive folder, the ticket, the receipt, the email — scattered across a dozen apps and searched for at the worst moment. The fix: Claude files each as a typed content item. Every type renders the same way wherever it lands — a glanceable card in Now, a block inside a Hub, and a full Detail view on tap. The use case decides the data it carries and the trigger that decides when it shows.
