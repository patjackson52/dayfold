One calm language for every write

Every two-way action — a checkbox, a card, a photo, a delete — shares one lifecycle: instant optimistic → saving → saved (silent), or offline — saved, will sync, or couldn't save — will retry. No per-action spinners. No error dialogs. The micro-affordance is a hairline, a quiet glyph, a dimmed tint — never a blocking overlay. This vocabulary is reused by every screen in this set.

boltOptimistic = the value commits to the screen the instant you tap; the sync state lives quietly at the edge.
