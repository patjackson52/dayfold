Brand identity · the day, folded into one place

From hearth to fold

The product gathers your day and tucks it away until it matters — then a card unfolds into the full thing you need. Dayfold names that motion. The mark is a content card with a turned corner: a fold you can open. Same warm M3 Expressive palette as before — only the name and the mark change.
